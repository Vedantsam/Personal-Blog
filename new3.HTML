Madara was born on December 24 and was a childhood friend and rival of Hashirama Senju, the first Hokage. The two shared a dream of creating a peaceful village, but their differing views on how to achieve this led to a power struggle. Madara’s intense rivalry with Hashirama stemmed from his envy of Hashirama’s natural talent and his own perceived inadequacies.
